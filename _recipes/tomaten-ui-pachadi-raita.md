Tomaten-ui pachadi (raita)

-   1.5 cup yoghurt (ong 370 ml)
-   3 tomaten
-   1 kleine ui
-   2 groene chilipepers
-   2 tbs kokos
-   1 tsp suiker
-   2 tbs verse koriander
-   1 tsp garam masala

Klop de yoghurt, meng de rest erdoorheen.
